Data Scientist with a Business Management Background, a strong desire to be a positive force in the world through my knowledge and skills. Highly analytical thinker with demonstrated talent for identifying, scrutinizing, analyzing, improving, and streamlining complex work processes.

I recently started working as a Data Scientist/Data Engineer at Medwise. My work mainly revolves around extracting Data from a number of different UK and Internationally accredited websites. We collect medical conditions/diseases and sicknesses, commonly asked Questions & Answers using web scraping and other tools and compile all this information into JATS format in order to make storing, accessing and adding to it with ease.

The end goal was to allow this data to be format ready to be used for Machine Learning and Deep Learning in order to produce a Query-Answering Software to help aid and speed a doctor/medical experts decision making.
