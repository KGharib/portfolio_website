My Projects

Carribean Holiday Tour

Hired by a Caribbean Holiday Operator who is looking to maximize customer conversion given limited marketing budget. The Data is from a taxonomy quiz where users choose images which corresponds to tags. The aim is to find those users who are a good fit and are likely to purchase a caribbean holiday trip to focus our advertising to.



Predicting Life Expectancy in counties in the USA

As part of a consulting group, I was tasked with analyzing the results of a questionnaire and finding which Social environmental factor affected the life expectancy in different counties in the USA. The information would then be used further to help improve the life expectancy in each of those states by targeting those social environmental factors and focus on developing and improving them.



NLP- Predicting Sentiment on Amazon Reviews

Based on a large Amazon product and service review dataset, my aim was to be able to predict whether a review is positive or negative based on the context of the textual review. The implications would be that it could be used in future uses to predict sentiment of products and services outside of Amazon.



Hypothesis Testing on NBA Season stats

NBA statistics Dataset which was found on kaggle. The goal was to answer 3 questions by applying statistical analysis and Hypothesis Testing. Examples of one of the questions was:

    - Can we see a significant difference in player performance when playing Home vs Away?



Predicting Wins and losses in Football Leagues

Based on a Dataset of Football season Statics I aimed to predict which teams would win and lose depending on a multitude of feature variables.
