More about me


How long have you been practicing Data Science for ?
I have been studying for around 2 years. I have applied my knowledge and skills in a number of different contexts such as personal projects, Quality Control Analysis, as well as my most recent role at Medwise with webscraping and development and research of Machine Learning tools.
Why are you interested in the medical industry ?
I think that the medical industry has one of the most massive movements of data when compared to any other industry. There is so much potenial to use that data for alot of good, which is why I am doing my internship with Medwise, the uses for the data is almost endless and I continually see so many ways that the implementation of Data Science can be a powerful and game changing tool to really bring about alot of good and make a difference.
What kind of Machine Learning projects do you enjoy doing ?
I love doing projects that can make an actual difference. Where the results have real life applications and the results of which can be used after and for further change. I like to see the end goal of any Machine learning projects I do be more than just the output or accuracy, when the output and conclusion can be used for more I feel more passionate and interested.
Why and How did you make this website ?
I made this website to present some of my skills and knowledge, as well as to have a single place where people can access all of my details and contact me easily. I used Python Flask to develop and build the website alonside using my knowledge and tools such as Bootstrap, HTML, CSS and JavaScript to build the website.
